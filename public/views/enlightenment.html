Enlightenment


As described in the Websters Dictionary, enlightenment refers to the state of having knowledge or understanding : the act of giving someone knowledge or understanding.


The sole purpose of this blog is to increase my own knowledge and understanding towards our mind, body and spirit. I believe it is a part of my distinct calling to help illuminate the world of darkness with this inspiring information I am gaining. I am not perfect, nor will I ever be, but I do believe if I strive to gain more insight towards other people's experiences I will help illuminate their light into the world.

As you scroll through my blog you will read some of my favorite quotes, scriptures, and interviews, from inspiring foodies, fitness junkies and successful leaders. Who knows -- you might be the next story I feature!
