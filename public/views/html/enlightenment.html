Enlightenment

As described in the Websters Dictionary Enlightenment refers to the state of having knowledge or understanding : the act of giving someone knowledge or understanding.

The soul purpose of this Blog is to increase my own knowledge and understanding towards our mind, body and spirit. I believe it is apart of my distinct calling to help illuminate the world of darkness with this inspiring information I am gaining. I am not perfect, nor will I ever be, but I do believe If I strive to gain more insight towards other people's experiences I will help illuminate their light into the world.

As you scroll through my blog you will read some of my favorite quotes, scriptures, and interviews; from inspiring foodies, fitness junkies and successful leaders. Who knows you might be the next story I feature!
