Chocolate Chip Zucchini Muffins

Makes 12 Regular Size Muffins or 30 Mini Muffins

  • 2 ripe Bananas
  • 1 Cup grated Zucchini
  • 2 egg whites
  • 2 Tbs unsweetened Applesauce
  • 1/4 Cup honey or raw Maple Syrup
  • 1 tsp Pure Vanilla
  • 1/3 Cup Almond Milk mixed with one scoop of Vanilla Shakeology (or plant protein powder)
  • 1 tsp coconut oil
  • 1 1/4 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 cup dark chocolate chips
  • 1 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 1/2 cup oat flour or Einkorn flour (Young Living Ancient Grains)

Blend all of the wet ingredients together first, (I use my Vitamix). Then, in a bowl mix all of the remaining ingredients together. Bake at 350 for 12-15 minutes.
